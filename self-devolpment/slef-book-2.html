Rich Dad Poor Dad

by Robert Kiyosaki

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts the financial philosophies of two father figures: his biological "Poor Dad" and his best friend's "Rich Dad." "Poor Dad" values formal education, job security, and steady income, reflecting a conventional path to financial stability. In contrast, "Rich Dad" champions financial independence through entrepreneurship, investments, and financial education. He teaches Kiyosaki to understand assets, liabilities, and the importance of taking risks to build wealth. The book advocates for financial literacy and proactive wealth-building, offering readers an alternative approach to achieving financial success.
